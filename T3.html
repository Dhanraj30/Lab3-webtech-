Grilled Cheese Sandwich with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Tomato

Instructions

1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
2. Cook bacon until crispy.
3. Remove bacon from skillet and set aside.
4. Place bread in skillet and top with cheese, bacon, and tomato.
5. Cook sandwich until cheese is melted and bread is golden brown.
6. Serve and enjoy!
